Frequently asked

Does XMetric work offline?
Yes. All conversions and calculators run entirely on your device — no internet connection is required.

Why does a value look different than I expect?
Results are rounded for display (up to 6 significant digits, switching to scientific notation for very large or very small numbers). The underlying calculation uses full precision.

How do I pin a conversion to Favorites?
Open any conversion and tap the star icon in the top-right corner. Pinned favorites also appear in the home screen widget.

The home screen widget isn't showing my favorites.
Make sure you've pinned at least one favorite in the app, then long-press the widget and choose “Edit Widget” to confirm it's linked to XMetric. If it still doesn't update, removing and re-adding the widget usually fixes it.

Can I use “Convert 30 psi to bar” with Siri?
Yes — XMetric adds a “Convert Units” action to Siri and the Shortcuts app. Say “Convert units with XMetric” and Siri will ask for the value and units, or build a fully parameterized shortcut in the Shortcuts app.

I found an incorrect conversion factor.
Please email us the category and units involved — accuracy matters and we'll fix it quickly.
